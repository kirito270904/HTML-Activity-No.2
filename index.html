Event Overview ★

BS Computer Science students are encouraged to participate in activities that strengthen programming, problem solving, teamwork, and innovation. This event provides opportunities to apply classroom knowledge to real world challenges through coding competitions, technology exhibits, workshops, and collaborative projects.

Throughout the week, students will improve their technical skills, explore new technologies, and connect with fellow aspiring software developers, cybersecurity specialists, data analysts, and CS professionals. ✓
